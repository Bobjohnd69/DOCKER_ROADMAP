SOME MISSING CONCEPT IN DOCKER

Depend on the book "Docker: Up & Running" (by Sean P. Kane & Karl Matthias) and  Operating System Concepts (by Peter Baer Galvin, Greg Gagne, Abraham Silberschatz) the isolation of Docker is quite different due to the way it isolate the data and structure.
